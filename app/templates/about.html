Meet Reliable Walks

Why Reliable Walks?

I founded Reliable Walks to give pet parents the freedom to focus on work, travel, and family—knowing their dog is experiencing the same level of devotion they give at home.

From gentle puppies to confident seniors, I customise pace, distance, and enrichment to match each personality.

Calm & Connected

Scent-led exploration, decompression breaks, and calm hand signals keep walks relaxing and confidence-building.

Safety First

GPS-tracked routes, double-clip leads, and emergency plans mean your companion is protected at every step.

Transparent Updates

Expect playful photos, detailed summaries, and honest feedback after every session.
